Welcome to Zephyr 2022-23! Join us for an exciting range of events designed to challenge and entertain you. From technical workshops to fun games, there's something for everyone. Explore below to find out more about each event.

Zephyr 2022-23 Events

Garba Workshop

The Garba Workshop offers a unique opportunity to delve into the rich world of Garba, a traditional Indian dance form celebrated during Navaratri. Led by skilled instructors, this workshop provides participants with an immersive experience, where they'll learn the intricate footwork, graceful hand gestures, and spirited movements that define Garba.

Learn More

Tech Hunt

Tech Hunt challenges participants to solve coding puzzles and unlock new levels. Each solved challenge reveals the location of the next PC, where players engage in games and coding to advance. Combining coding with strategy, this event takes participants through a series of tech challenges, testing problem-solving skills and teamwork at every stage.

Learn More

Bulls Eye

Bulls Eye is a two-stage event where participants first toss small plastic balls into a cup in a mini basketball game. In the second stage, they use a Nerf gun to knock ) down plastic glasses from a table. Precision and quick reflexes are key to winning this exciting challenge.

Learn More

COD Mobile

Bring your team and play the team death match! Showcase your gaming skills and rise to the top to win this event!

Learn More

Cricket Auction

Have you wondered what you would do if you get a chance to build your own IPL team? Calling all Cricket fans for Zephyr 2022's Cricket Auction! Raise the bid and seal the deal!

Learn More

Neon Cricket

Join us in this innovative cricket event - all equipment will be neon and the match will be played in a dark room. Have a blast with your team and experience this new idea!

Learn More

Rink Football

A unique way to play football, in which the game is played with 5 players and 2 substitutes. Bring your friends and have a fun time at this interesting sports event!
